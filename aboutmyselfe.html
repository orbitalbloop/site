About me

My name is Christopher, online I go by orbitaldot or simply Orb. Currently I'm doing a bachelor's degree in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence.

My interests lie in the following mediums:

- Traditional art (for as long as i can remember lol)
- Digital art (since 2011)
- Game development & programming (since 2013)
- Music composition (since 2016)


My tools:

Music:
For music-making, I use FL Studio 12. 99% of my instrumentation comes from a variety of soundfonts, mainly ones from older video games and general midi banks.

Art:
For digital art, I use Clip Studio Paint Pro (with a key provided by the very generous ChemicalSushi, give them a follow) and a Wacom Bamboo CTL-460.
For pixel art, I use FireAlpaca, even though it's not intended for that use...
For traditional art, I use, uh ... pen and paper.

Programming
