Games

Paynplayne (2019)

My Ludum Dare submission for April 2019.

You play as a creature whose body can turn pain into gold, which you can use to purchase items from your creator in order to deliver a letter to her sister.

Not too proud of the game design aspects, but it's still good enough for a 48 hour gamejam submission.

A World In Her Eye (2017)

My Ludum Dare submission for April 2017.

You play as Qora, who lives on a small planet in an almost empty universe. Occasionally, a planet passes by your own. In AWIHE, you have a limited amount of time to explore this passing planet (unless you play calm mode lol)
